# Market Thesis

## Why This Category Can Become Large

The market opportunity behind Leviathan is not limited to token screening. It sits inside a broader transition:

- software is becoming autonomous
- agents are beginning to request services, screen assets, and move capital directly
- machine-to-machine payments are becoming realistic on Solana
- the missing layer is not more dashboards, but decision and execution legitimacy

## Why Now

### Generative AI is creating the economic base

McKinsey estimates generative AI could generate **$2.6T-$4.4T** in annual economic value across industries.

If even a small share of that value is mediated by agents interacting with financial systems, the need for machine-native risk and policy infrastructure grows quickly.

### Solana is actively enabling agent commerce

Solana now documents and promotes agentic payments and x402-based machine payments as a real application layer.

That matters because once agents can pay for:

- data
- analysis
- APIs
- execution services
- settlement

then pre-trade decisions, execution policy, and accountability become infrastructure requirements rather than optional analytics.

## What Leviathan Sells Into

Leviathan can support several downstream markets:

- agent trading and allocation systems
- machine-assisted diligence for funds and operators
- listing review and token screening workflows
- controlled machine spending and future agent payments
- institutional Solana infrastructure where evidence must be usable and execution must be bounded
